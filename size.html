HOW TO MEASURE YOUR GARMENT SIZE ?

See the video tutorial above. You only need an Indian or Indo-western dress that fits you well, and a measuring tape or ruler. You can aslo see the steps written below.

SIZE GUIDE

M (38) 38 36 40
L (40) 40 38 42
XL (42) 42 40 44
XL (44) 44 426 4

01. FIRST, THE CHEST.


Measure the dress from armpit to armpit on one side.

Double this number. This is your Chest size. In the diagram above, the 'Armpit to Armpit' length is 19 inches. Therefore, this garment's chest size is 19 X 2 = 38 inches.

02. THEN, WAIST & HIPS.

Repeat Step 1 for waist & hips. Waist is usually one inch above the navel. Hip is the widest part of the kurti below the waist. In the diagram above, the Waist will measure 18 x 2 =36 inches and the Hips will measure 20 x 2 = 40 inches. You're done!

03. FINAL SUGGESTIONS.

  • If you fall between sizes always go for the larger size. Our designs across all collections are easily alterable.
  • Watch the video tutorial above. It's simple and short. If you need any assistance, please don't hesitate to contact us.
