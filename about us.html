ABOUT SHOPPER

Shopper is the culmination of a decade old journey into fashion, style, colors and fabric. It’s your feel good
for the day, every day.
The philosophy of the brand is simple: Bring international contemporary designs and fashion to the Indian men, with a distinct ethnic touch. Through the use of vibrant colors, contrasting it with subtle & elegant designs, and basing it in rich, woven fabrics (cotton, silk & khadi), Shopper wants to blur the lines between casual and chic.

The brand is the brainchild of Shopper, an international merchandiser by profession and a pioneer in bringing local artisan talent to international fashion houses.
